Pasta With Veggies and Vegan Sausage

This is my favorite fresh pasta sauce to make. The choice of pasta is up to you, I usually use ziti.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of pasta, your choice
  • one can of diced tomatoes
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1/3 borwn onion
  • dried chili pepper flakes
  • 5 or 6 mushrooms
  • 1 zucchini
  • 1 vegan sausage link
  • olive oil

Steps

  • Add two tabelspoons of olive oil and half a teaspoon of chili flakes to a large cooking pot and warm over a medium flame
  • Chop onion and garlic and add to pot
  • When onion and garlic have become translucent add diced tomatoes (with juice)
  • Slice mushrooms and add to pot
  • Slice zucchini to preferred size and add to pot
  • Slice sausage link to preferred size and add to pot
  • Cover pot and simmer until zucchini is tender
  • In a separate pot bring salted water to a boil, add pasta of choice, cook until done, drain
  • If timed properly the past will be finished cooking about the same time as the sauce and can be added right into the pot, mixed, and served
