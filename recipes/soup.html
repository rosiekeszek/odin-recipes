Soup

Soup is delicious and really great for you. You can really put just about anything in soup. This is a simple recipe for a soup my grandmother used to make. I hope you enjoy it!

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • onion
  • garlic
  • fresh or dried dill
  • carrots
  • celery
  • potatoes
  • barley
  • vegetable stock

Steps

  • Pour two tablespoons of olive oil into a pan and heat over a medium flame.
  • Chop onions and garlic and sautee until translucent.
  • Chop and add carrots.
  • Chop and add celergy.
  • Chop and add potatoes.
  • Add enough vegetable stock to completely cover all the vegetables in the pan.
  • Add a teaspoon of dill.
  • Add a handful of barley.
  • Cook until barley is done.
