Salad

A salad is a lovely addition to any meal. Adding nuts and berries to a green salad makes it more interesting and nutritious.

Ingredients

  • baby herb mixed greens
  • sliced and toasted almonds
  • chopped and toasted walnuts
  • dried cranberries
  • white cheddar cheese shredded
  • olive oil
  • rice vinegar

Steps

  • grab a big bowl
  • put greens, nuts and cranberries in the bowl
  • mix oil and vinegar in a smaller bowl
  • lightly dress salad with oil and vinegar
  • gently toss salad to mix and distribute dressing
  • sprinkle with shredded cheese
